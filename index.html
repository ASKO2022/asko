Group courses
  • The schedule of the courses may vary, but usually the courses take place at least twice a week, for 60-90 minutes per lesson.
  • Courses may be held in the morning, afternoon or evening to fit participants' schedules.
  • There can be up to 10-15 people in the group to ensure that each student receives enough attention from the teacher and the opportunity to communicate with all group members.
Individual courses
  • Courses can take place at least twice a week, 60-90 minutes per lesson. Class times can be adjusted according to your progress and language skill level.
  • In individual courses, students receive more intensive and personalized learning. The teacher can focus on those aspects of the language that cause the most difficulty for the student and develop an individualized learning plan that will be most effective for achieving specific goals.
  • At the end of the course, the student may receive a certificate of course completion, which can be useful when applying to educational institutions or when looking for a job.
